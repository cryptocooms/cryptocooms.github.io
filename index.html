What are CryptoCooms?
They are pure expressions of euphoria, the consequence of seeing green candles on a price graph: elation elicited by exponential price gains. CryptoCooms are a surreal representation of the beauty, diversity, and history of the global cryptocurrency ecosystem: a venerable ecology of projects and the people who create and participate in them.
...but what are they really?
CryptoCooms are gobs of coom collectible works of art with proof of ownership stored on the Tezos blockchain in the form of FA2 tokens, otherwise known as NFTs (Non-fungible tokens). The collection is a limited edition of only sixty-nine pieces. Each CryptoCoom is a carefully handcrafted masterwork of the highest quality. High-resolution copies of the works themselves, accessible to the sixty-nine owners, are hosted in a decentralized manner using a technology known as the Interplanetary File System, or IPFS.
How can I get one?
You can buy and sell NFTs on decentralized marketplaces. CryptoCooms are now traded on the Teia platform. To buy one, you will first need to purchase some Tezos tokens on an exchange, like Kraken. Then, you will need to send those tokens to a Tezos wallet. Teia reccomends using Kukai. You can then connect the wallet to the Teia decentralized app.
